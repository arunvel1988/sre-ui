Scaling ensures that systems can handle increased load, traffic, and data while maintaining performance and reliability.

Why Scaling Matters

  • Supports business growth without degrading user experience.
  • Prepares infrastructure for predictable and unpredictable demand spikes.
  • Optimizes resource usage to balance cost and performance.

Best Practices

  • Ensure monitoring and alerting scale alongside infrastructure.

Scaling Tools & Technologies

  • AWS Auto Scaling / Azure VM Scale Sets / GCP Instance Groups.
  • CDNs for content scaling.
